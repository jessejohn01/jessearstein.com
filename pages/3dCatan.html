I love Catan and I love 3d printing. This project is an amalgamation of the two. After seeing the 3d models from here, I had to upgrade my Prusa MK3S 3d printer to print in multiple colors. A firmware upgrade here and hours of assembly later, I had my first multiple color print.

I intially made the tiles as the original creator intended but I found that gluing magnets in place was a tedious process. I altered the design to pause my 3d printer at a specific layer and allow me to drop in my magnets into the print. This made the process much simpler and allowed for my tiles to print in one piece instead of multiple.
